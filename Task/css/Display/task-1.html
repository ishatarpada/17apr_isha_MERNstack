Sanjeev Kapoor

Sanjeev Kapoor is the most famous and commended chef in India. He is also an entrepreneur and a television personality.

The Indian government honored him with the Padma Shri award in 2017.

He won the ITA Award for Popular Chef and Entrepreneur in 2015.

Vikas Khanna

Vikas Khanna was born on 14 November 1971 in Amritsar, Punjab, India. He is an Indian chef, restaurateur, cookbook writer, filmmaker.

In 2012, he won the GQ India Man of the Year Award given by GQ Magazine.

In 2012, he got the place on the cover page of Men's Health India Magazine.

Ranveer Brar

Ranveer Brar is an Indian chef, TV & digital celebrity, judge, host, author, restaurateur, food film producer, and also benefactor.

He is an honorary member of the James Beard Foundation.

In 2018, Ranveer Brar was awarded the LFEGA's Food Entertainer of the Year.

What is the use of Void (0)?

➛ void(0) is often used to prevent the browser from navigating to a new page when clicking on a link or button.
➛ void(0) return undefined as a primitive value..
➛It's typically used as the value of the href attribute in an anchor () tag or as the parameter to the href attribute of a link.

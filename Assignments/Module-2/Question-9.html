Which property controls the image scroll in the background?

  ➩The background-attachment property control background image scrolls with the full of the page, or is fixed.

☣ Syntax:-

  ➩background-attachment: scroll|fixed;

  • scroll

      ➩ The background image will scroll with the page. This is default

  • fixed

      ➩ The background image will not scroll with the page

background-attachment: fixed;

Helloo Good Morning

background-attachment: scroll;

Helloo Good Morning

Question-6 How can the background color of an element be changed?

The background-color property sets the background color of an element.

background-color: navy;
background-color: #000072;
background-color: rgb(47, 47, 166);
background-color: rgba(14, 30, 134, 0.532);
hsl(240, 45%, 26%);
background-color: hsla(240, 45%, 26%,0.5);
background : linear-gradient(to right, deeppink , darkviolet);
background : redial-gradient(to right, deeppink , darkviolet);

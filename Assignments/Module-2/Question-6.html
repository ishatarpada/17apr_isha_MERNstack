Question-6 How can the background color of an element be changed?

The background-color property sets the background color of an element.

background-color: darkslategray;
background-color: #2f4f4f;
background-color: rgb(47, 79, 79);
background-color: rgba(47, 79, 79, 0.571)
hsl(180, 25%, 25%);
background-color: hsla(180, 45%, 26%, 0.354);
background : linear-gradient(to right , #0e4745 , #6ba6a4);
background : redial-gradient(teal ,darkslategray );

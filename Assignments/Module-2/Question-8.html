Question-8 What is the use of the background-position property?

☣   The background-position property used for give the position of a background image.

✦   Syntax:-
      ☣ background-position: value;

    • left top
    • left center
    • left bottom
    • right top
    • right center
    • right bottom
    • center top
    • center center
    • center bottom

  • x% y%

  • xpos ypos

⇥   the background image will be positioned top left or top right or top center .
    ☣background-position: top left / top right / top center ;

Question-5 What do you understand by CSS opacity?

✒ Sets the opacity level for the image.
✒ The opacity-level describes the transparency-level,
✒ 0% is completely transparent.
✒ 100% (1) is default and display the original image.

❋Example:-

filter: opacity(0%);


filter: opacity(25%);


filter: opacity(50%);


filter: opacity(75%);


filter: opacity(100%);

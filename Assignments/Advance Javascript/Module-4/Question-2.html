Question-2 What is Promise?

  1. Promises are a mechanism in JavaScript that allows you to work with asynchronous operations more easily.
  2. They are used to handle asynchronous tasks such as fetching data from a server, reading files, or executing timeouts.
  3. Promises can be in one of three states:
    • Pending: Initial state when the Promise is created and waiting for the operation to complete.
    • Fulfilled (Resolved): The operation completed successfully, and the Promise has a resolved value.
    • Rejected: The operation failed, and the Promise has a reason for the failure.

What is a bootstrap card and how would you create one

➺ In Bootstrap 5, a card is a flexible and extensible content container.

➺ It allows you to display content like text, images, links, and more in a structured and visually appealing manner.

➺ Cards are often used to present information in a concise and organized way.

➺ To create a Bootstrap 5 card, you can use the card class along with other card-related classes.

➺ It includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors, and powerful display options.

Semantic element in HTML5? 

                    ⤞ In HTML5 , There are two types of tags.
                        1. semantic tag
                            ⤞ semantic elements are describe the structure of content on a web page.
                            ⤞ example :-  <nav></nav> , <footer></footer> , <header></header>
                        2. content tag 
                            ⤞ content type of  elements are describe content on a web page.
                            ⤞ example :-  <Address></Address> , <audio></audio> , <video></video>
